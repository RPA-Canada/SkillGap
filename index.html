📋 How to Use

  1. Upload a Project Document: This can be an RFP, project scope, job description, or any text that describes the skills needed for your project.
  2. Upload Employee Skills CSV: A CSV file with two columns: employee_name and skill (one skill per row).
  3. Click Analyze: The AI will extract skills from your document and compare them against your workforce.
